What do I love the most about weddings? More than anything is capturing honest moments, filled with atmosphere, excitement, love and emotion. I want you to look back at your photographs and recall real memories of your day.

Thanks for visiting my page! I’m Jude - I’m a female btw - my name often confuses the issue! I’m married with two girls and live just outside of Omagh, Co Tyrone. I’ve been shooting weddings for over 8 years now and photography has been my full time profession since 2007.

So a little bit about my wedding photography, my style! My main approach is for you to enjoy your day to the full, I strongly believe the images you will love most are the meaningful ones - where you are having the best day ever celebrating with the most important people in your life! Yes I still guide you through the day (making sure you’re on time), setup family and bridal party photographs but as much as possible I stand back and capture what is happening, providing you with a true story of your day. My photojournalistic style is mixed with relaxed and simple couple portraits to provide beautiful, authentic photographs. I document every part of your day from the fine details, family group photographs to the guest candids. I am passionate about photography and providing you excellent customer service.
