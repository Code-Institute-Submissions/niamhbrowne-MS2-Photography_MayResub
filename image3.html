Christmas Wedding
Catherine & Noel

Catherine and Noel were the first of my Christmas 2020 weddings. I cannot imagine the strength it took to stick with their date as they had the full 9 months wondering if they would be allowed to get married, watching so many changes in restrictions. With Northern Ireland in their 2nd lockdown, receptions were not allowed but determined to get married they rearranged the venue and headed South to Cavan for an intimate reception following their service in Enniskillen.
For photographs we visited the beautiful ruins of Monea Castle and although we were caught in a shower the sun returned and we were able to enjoy a bit of a sunset. A truly beautiful day, congratulations x
