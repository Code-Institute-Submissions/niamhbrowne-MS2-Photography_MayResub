An Grianan Hotel Wedding
Siobhan & Aidan

Siobhan and Aidan were blessed with exceptional weather for their September wedding. Although the run up to their day was uncertain with not knowing how restrictions would affect them they decided they just wanted to get married regardless. Lucky enough they were able to go ahead with 50 guests and were as strange as it sounds, were one of my larger weddings of 2020. When Siobhan walked down the aisle I loved Aidan’s expression, a visible ‘wow’ is clear in the photograph! Their smiles said it all as they left the church, just happy they were finally married. For our photographs we headed to Grianan of Aileach, an amazing scenic spot close to the hotel. Definitely worth a visit if weather is on your side! A beautiful day with two lovely families x
